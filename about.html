About

My Story

I'm all about systems. I simplify and organize things for better big-picture efficiency, because I know the domino effect of a good system can be huge. Working in the healthcare industry left a lot to be desired in terms of efficiency, and while I did eventually design and implement some solutions during my time in strategic planning, my hunger for better systems has ultimately driven me towards user experience design. My long-term goal is to design more than just apps and websites, because our non-digital lives are equally as deserving of efficient systems as our digital lives are.

  • And of course, a well-rounded set of UX design skills.
