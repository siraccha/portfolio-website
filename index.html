Gone are the days of choosing between form and function.

I'm Sarah, a UX designer and illustrator.

In the rush to make survival and advancement possible, we can’t forget to make it all worthwhile, too. By designing beautiful, human-centered systems that encourage healthy mindsets and minimize things that don’t add to quality of life, we can find more enjoyment in our everyday lives and make more room for the things that matter - things like cultivating meaningful relationships, investing in personal growth, and adding to the natural beauty of our world. That’s why I do what I do.

My Work

Raft - the app for Florida's beaches

Raft is a responsive web app that provides beachgoers and water athletes with the data they need in a digestible and visually pleasing format, making it easier to stay safe and have fun at the beach. My role in this project was general - I did everything from research and IA to UI and interaction design.

View Project

NorthWest Fruit - a website and member portal for a community orchard

The NorthWest Fruit website was a team effort aimed at helping a sustainable public orchard increase its community involvement and attract more volunteers. My role in this project was design-based - I synthesized data from the research team to make evidence-based decisions, resulting in intuitive task-flows and enticing, easy-to-digest visuals.
