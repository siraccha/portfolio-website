What I'm All About

In the rush to make survival and advancement possible, we can't forget to make it all worthwhile, too. By enacting beautiful and human-centered systems that minimize deadweight and encourage healthy habits, we can find more enjoyment in our daily lives and make more time and energy for the things that matter. That's why I do what I do.

I'm all about systems. I simplify and organize things for better big-picture efficiency, because I know the domino effect of a good system can be huge. Working in the healthcare industry left a lot to be desired in terms of efficiency, and while I did eventually design and implement some solutions during my time in strategic planning, my hunger for better systems has ultimately driven me towards user experience design. In the long term, I'd love to explore different ways of applying the UX design approach to more than just apps and websites, because our non-digital lives are equally as deserving of efficient, human-centered systems as our digital lives are.
